About Us

A Better Way to Serve the Lumber Industry

Scott Lumber Packaging was founded in 2005 by Scott Galbraith after years of working in the industry and seeing a better way to serve customers. By cutting out middlemen and unnecessary management layers, we deliver a stronger product at a competitive price — with lead times the competition can't touch.

In 2020, Scott's son Will Galbraith took over as CEO and continues to run the business with the same hands-on philosophy: answer every call, ship every order fast, and never let a customer run out of wrap. We're a small team of five, and every one of us is critical to keeping your operation moving.
